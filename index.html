CRITTER CARE

  • Grow in clean, filtered water in a large bowl or Tupperware container. DO NOT cover.
  • Once grown to desired size, keep dry and in a cool dry place for storage/safe keeping.
  • Grow Pets begin to shrink at about 28 days from desired growth. To maintain consistent
    growth and size, routinely place your grow creature in clean filtered water.
    The more your grow pet grows,
    the longer it stays to desired size.
